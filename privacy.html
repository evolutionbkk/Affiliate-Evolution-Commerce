Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Affiliate Studio (the "App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our content scheduling and publishing tool that integrates with TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the information needed to provide our service, including your TikTok profile basics (username, display name, avatar) and the authorization tokens required to upload and publish content on your behalf. We also store the videos, images, captions, and scheduling settings that you create within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to: (a) authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0; (b) upload, schedule, and publish the content you choose; and (c) display your profile so you can confirm which account you are posting to. We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Authorization tokens and content are stored securely and used only to carry out actions you explicitly request. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which revokes our access. Locally created drafts and schedules are stored on your own device.

4. Sharing of Information

We share information with TikTok only as required to perform the actions you request through the official TikTok API. We do not share your information with any other third parties except as required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is connected. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Disconnecting your TikTok account removes our stored access tokens.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information, and withdraw consent for data processing, at any time by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
