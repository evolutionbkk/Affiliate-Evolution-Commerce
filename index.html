Transparency

TikTok permissions we use — and why

Affiliate Studio only requests the scopes required to do exactly what you ask. Nothing more.

user.info.basic

Read your basic profile (open ID, avatar) so you can confirm which TikTok account you're posting to.

user.info.profile

Show your display name and username in the dashboard for a clear posting experience.

video.upload

Upload your video to your TikTok account — sent to your drafts/inbox for you to review and finish.

video.publish

Publish content directly to your profile once your app is approved for direct posting.

We never post without your action, never add watermarks to your content, and never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Questions

Frequently asked

What does Affiliate Studio do?

It's a dashboard for TikTok affiliate creators. You can create and schedule posts, publish them to TikTok through the official Content Posting API, scan products by commission and trend, and analyze the market with Market Radar.

How do I sign in?

You sign in with TikTok's official Login Kit (OAuth 2.0). There is no separate username or password — authentication is handled entirely by TikTok, and you can disconnect at any time to revoke access.

Does it post to my account automatically?

Only when you choose to. In draft mode, your clip is uploaded to your TikTok inbox and you finish publishing inside the TikTok app, keeping full control over caption and privacy.

Is my data safe?

We only request the minimum TikTok permissions needed, use them solely to carry out actions you request, and never sell your data. Drafts and schedules are stored on your own device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How much does it cost?

You can connect your TikTok account and explore the dashboard at no cost. Contact us for details on availability in your region.
